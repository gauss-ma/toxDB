Sobre ToxDB

ToxDB has a dual nature.

Acá se podría poner un texto sobre el proyecto, alcances, contriubudores, y esas cosas. Al mismo tiempo, the font features friendly and open curves. While some grotesks distort their letterforms to force a rigid rhythm, Roboto doesn’t compromise, allowing letters to be settled into their natural width. This makes for a more natural reading rhythm more commonly found in humanist and serif types.

ToxDB es un proyecto de software libre y gratuito .
